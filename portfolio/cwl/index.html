Welcome

Our mission is to provide quality service to our clients and carriers, while upholding a high level of integrity. Transporting commodities throughout the country safely and in a timely fashion shouldn't be considered a bonus, but should be a baseline standard. This is our standard and we strive to make transporting goods a painless experience for the clients and carriers we work with.

Here at Crosswind Logistics we maintain close relationships with established carriers to provide our clients with fast response times and reliable goods transportation services. When we work with new carriers, we take a close look at their records and avoid ones which fall outside our standards for safety and responsibility. Carriers must meet the safety standards and regulations developed by the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration (FMCSA), on top of our own internal vetting process.

There are times when unforseen events can happen, such as bad weather, accidents or other unavoidable scenarios. When these events happen, we make sure to keep everyone informed and work hard to correct the issues at hand. We pride ourselves in being good problem solvers. We're straight shooters and have seen many difficult and interesting situations through to the end.

Whether you are a possible new client looking to have goods delivered or a carrier looking for goods to transport, we'll work hard to get the job done. Take a look at the services section to learn more about what we offer. If you have any questions, you can email us directly from the inqueries section.

Services

Our primary service offering is locating dependable delivery methods for clients. We are connected to a deep network of freight carriers and thus can offer the specifc type of truck a client may need: flatbeds, vans, reefers, or anything in-between.

Our services don't stop at finding our clients a truck. We keep track of trucks and keep our clients informed of their delivery progress. We make sure to be on top of service hours at shipping and receiving locations and make appointments for loading or unloading when necessary. We like everyone to be on the same page. This standard helps us solve problems quickly, maintain constant delivery success, and keep our clients and carriers operating efficiently.

We have several years of industry and seasonal expertise that enable us to make educated decisions and operate efficiently. We work with our clients and carriers to get the best rates, making sure everyone walks away from a delivery getting not only quality service, but fair pay as well.
